ABOUT

Hello everyone, my name is Jacob Ringer! I am a ​highly-skilled, team-oriented programmer and designer. My goal is to provide only the best work with whoever I am working with, and I strive to continue learning more ways to better my skills in programming.

I graduated from Indiana University in the Spring of 2020, Majoring in Game Design with a specialization in programming. Within the game design program of the university, I have worked with several teams on many different projects. I have been through both the successes and failures of developing a game, and have learned immensely on what it takes to work on a successful game with a team.

Being in this field, I always make sure to continue learning new ways to better my work every single day. Technology has been changing and growing rapidly, so it’s essential to be a quick learner. There will always be something new I can learn or improve upon.

I would love to talk about job opportunities, my past projects, or anything else that comes to mind!
